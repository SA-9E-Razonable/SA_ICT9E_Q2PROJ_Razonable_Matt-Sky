Ingredients

— 1 kilo pork ribs — 2 cups sitaw
— 2 small eggplants — 5 pcs okra
— 1 bunch kangkong — 3 siling labuyo
— 1 stripped ginger — 2 red onions
— 1 tbsp. fish sauce — 1/2 kilo guavas
— 3 pcs taro root — 2 quartered tomatoes
  1. In a medium sized stainless-steel pot, boil 1.5 liters of tap water.
  2. Add the tomatoes, guavas, and okras, and cook for around 30-40 minutes.
  3. Add the onions, gabi, siling labuyo, & ginger (Cook for 8 more minutes)
  4. Lastly, add in the kangkong and simmer for 1 more minute.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
