ABOUT

Home Cooked-Goodness was established because of the owner’s loved for his grandmother’s home cooked food. Since he lived with his grandmother’s ancestral home, he learned all her recipes and serve it whenever he had visitors. His friends liked his cooking so much, so they always requested him to cook for every birthdays. He then thought "why not start an online business. Thus the birth of Home Cooked-Goodness begun...
