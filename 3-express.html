Crispy Bicol Express

Main Course

Learn More â†’

1.Cook the pork belly in boiling water. (Set aside and rest after cooking)
2. Cook and mix the residual water with gata, alamang, chili, and ginger.
3. In another pan, saute your garlic and onion, and deep fry the pork belly.
4. Remove from the pan, slice, arrange in a plate, and top it with our sauce.
5. Serve hot and enjoy!

-3 lbs. pork belly
-Salt and pepper
-3 pcs green pepper
-8 pcs thai chili
-3 tbsp. alamang
-1/4 tsp. black pepper
-145 g ginataang gulay

-1pc onion
-1 pc ginger
-8 cups water
-3 cloves of garlic
-3 cups cooking oil
