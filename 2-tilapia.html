Pinaputok na Tilapia

Main Course

Learn More â†’

1.Preheat your oven to 375 F and rinse the tilapia well.
2. Make 2-3 slits across the body of the fish & season with 2 tbsp tamarind powder.
3. In a bowl, combine the tomatoes, onions, ginger, garlic. (Salt and pepper to taste)
4. Stuff the tilapia with all the fillings and bake for 20-25 minutes/ until cooked.
5. Serve hot and enjoy!

-2 pcs tomatoes
-Salt and pepper
-3 pcs calamansi
-2 tbsp butter
-2 cloves garlic
-2 pcs whole tilapia
-3 tbsp. tamarind powder

-1 pc ginger
-1 cup water
-1/4 cup soy sauce
