Ingredients

— 6 cups water — 1 lb bagnet
— 1 pc knor cube — 12 pcs shrimp
— 3 tbsp cooking oil — 1 pc carrot
— 1/2 lb egg noodles — 5 cloves of garlic
— 1/4 cup oyster sauce — 12 pcs snow peas
— 2 pcs bell pepper — 1/2 kilo cabbage
  1. Prepare the egg noodles by boiling 6 cups of water in a cooking pot.
  2. Add the Knorr Pork Cube and cook until the noodles are soft. Set aside.
  3. Sauté the onion, garlic, mushrooms, carrot, cabbage, snow peas, and shrimp.
  4. Add the cabbage, oyster sauce, and ¼ cup water. (Cook for 1 more minute)
  5. Mix, serve warm and enjoy!
