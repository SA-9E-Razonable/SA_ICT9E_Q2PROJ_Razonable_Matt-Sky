Bagnet Pancit Canton

Snacks

Learn More →

1.Prepare the egg noodles by boiling 6 cups of water in a cooking pot.
2.Add the Knorr Pork Cube and cook until the noodles are soft. Set aside.
3. Sauté the onion, garlic, mushrooms, carrot, cabbage, snow peas, and shrimp.
4. Add the Nappa cabbage, oyster sauce, and ¼ cup water. (Cook for 1 more minute)
5. Mix, serve warm, and enjoy!

-6 cups water
-1 pc knor cube
-3 tbsp cooking oil
-1/2 lb egg noodles
-1/4 cup oyster sauce
-2 pcs bell pepper
-1/2 kilo Nappa cabbage

-1 lb bagnet
-12 pcs shrimp
-1 pc carrot
-5 cloves garlic
-12 pcs snow peas
