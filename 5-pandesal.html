Ingredients

— 250 grams cheese — 400 grams bread flour
— 1 cup squash — 70 grams butter
— 2 tsp dry yeast — 200 ml milk
— 1/4 cup bread crumbs — 1 tsp salt
— 60 grams white sugar

  1. In a large bowl, combine the flour, milk powder, yeast, salt and coconut sugar.
  2. Melt the butter and water together, cool, and set aside. Mash up the squash.
  3. Mix the water, butter, and mashed squash for 10 minutes.
  4. Roll the dough into a ball add cheese and bake at 350F.
  5. Serve warm and enjoy!
