Ingredients

— 1 cup heavy cream
— 2 pcs siling labuyo
— 1 cup condensed milk
— 2 to 4 cups sweet strawberries
  1. Chop and grind the siling labuyo using a blender.
  2. Combine the heavy cream, coconut cream, condensed milk, & strawberries.
  3. Beat using an electric mixer until smooth and add 1 tea spoon of siling labuyo.
  4. Pour the mixture in an air tight container and cool for 4-8 hours.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
