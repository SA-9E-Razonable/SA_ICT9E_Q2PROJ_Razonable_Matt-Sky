Ingredients

— 4 cups water — 1/2 cup coconut cream
— condensed milk — 250 grams malagkit rice
— 1 cup ube halaya — 1 tsp salt
— 1 tsp ube flavoring — butter (optional)
— evaporated milk
  1. Pour the rice and water in a medium stockpot and cook over medium heat.
  2. Bring to a boil then lower to a simmer until rice is cooked (Stir regularly)
  3. Stir in the ube halaya, flavoring, coconut cream, and a pinch of salt, to taste.
  4. Ladle the champorado into bowls and top with evaporated/condensed milk.
  5. Serve warm and enjoy!
